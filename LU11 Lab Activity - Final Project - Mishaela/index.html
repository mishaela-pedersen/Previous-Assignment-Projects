Welcome to a Despair that's Outta This World!

Danganronpa just got a whole new twist in this fanmade game! Explore the U.S.S Symphonia, a state-of-the-art space station training facility where specially selected students train for the upcoming mission to populate a newly discovered planet.
At least that was what the original purpose USED to be. Now, thanks to an uninvited guest, it's the stage for a deadly game of life-or-death where the only way out, is to kill and get away with it!
Will you be able to find the mastermind in time?
Let the Killing Game begin!

Gameplay

  • Progress through a deep twisting plotline in visual novel format!
  • Find clues through point-and-click crime scene investigations!
  • Uncover the culprit in the class trials, and shoot down contradictions with Truth Bullets!
  • Battle the 'Blackened' in an action-packed turn-based battle system!
  • Get to know your classmates and build your relationships through Free Time Events!

The interstellar battle between hope and despair is on!
